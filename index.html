If you are looking for a place to store all the recipes that you like, you are in the right place!

A question that a lot of us face at some point is, WHAT SHOULD I COOK TODAY?When I ran out of ideas, I ask my mom for recipes, or I search them on the internet. Sometimes I saved some recipes on Facebook, Instagram or TikTok but when I want to use some of them, it took me a lot of time searching on each platform for the recipe that I want.

So here is the solution, a place where you can store your favorite recipes!
